find-a-masjid (fam)

The inspiration for fam came from many times where my family and I would go to a new city and not know where the nearest Masjid (Muslim place of worship) is. fam hopes to alleviate some of that frustration by displaying many of the larger Masajid (plural for Masjid) in the United States, and lets registered users review, create, and edit them!

Languages:

HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Technologies:

MongoDB Atlas, Express, Node.js, Bootstrap, Embedded JavaScript (EJS), MapBox, Cloudinary
